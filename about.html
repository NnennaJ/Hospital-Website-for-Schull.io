Welcome to Mercy Hospital

We Offer the Best Health care Services to Our Patients

Mercy Hospital provides acute healthcare services for people from all over the country and beyond.

The mission of Mercy Hospital is to Promote, Preserve and Restore individual and family health by providing eexpert medical and surgical care within an innovative and dignified environment.

Our services are delivered from our main hospital site in Abuja. Nigeria, and satellite outpatient facilities at Lagos and Port Harcourt.
